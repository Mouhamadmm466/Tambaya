# Kwari da Adana Hatsi

## Kwarin Gona (Crop Pests)

### Fara (Grasshoppers da Locusts)

Fara na daya daga cikin mafi yawan barazanar da ke shafar noma a Niger. Idan aka ga garken fara suna shirin sauka a gona, sanar da hukumar noma ta gunduma (Direction Régionale de l'Agriculture) da sauri. Ana amfani da magungunan kashe kwari (pesticides kamar malathion ko pyrethroid) lokacin da adadin fara ya yi yawa sosai. Shuka da wuri (farkon Yuni) na taimaka wajen guje wa lokacin da fara ke yawa sosai. Ana iya amfani da tarko-tarko da haske da daddare (light traps) don kashe fara a gonaki ƙanana.

### Tsutsar Tsiron Gero da Dawa (Stem Borers)

Tsutsa na shiga cikin tsiron gero da dawa daga sama, ta shiga cikin tsakiyar tsiro. Alama ta farko: ganyen tsakiya ya bushe (ana kira "dead heart"). Idan ka ga wannan, cire tsiron da aka kama ka lalata shi. Juyawa nau'o'in amfanin gona (crop rotation): shuka wake ko gyada a shekara ta gaba a wannan gona don rage adadin tsutsa.

### Tsutsar Hatsi a Ajiya (Grain Weevils)

Tsutsar hatsi (Sitophilus spp.) na ɗaya daga cikin mafi girman cutarwa a lokacin ajiya. Tsutsa na girma cikin tsaba kuma ba a ganinta sai an buɗe tsabar. Da zarar ta shiga cikin ajiya, na iya lalata duk hatsin cikin watanni. Guje wa shigar tsutsa ta hanyar:
- Tabbatar hatsi ya bushe sosai (ƙasa da 14% ruwa) kafin ajiya
- Kankame ajiya kafin amfani
- Hana ruwa da damshi shiga cikin ajiya

## Adana Hatsi da Inganci (Safe Grain Storage)

### Silos na Karfe (Metal Silos)

Silos na karfe su ne mafi kyawun hanyar adana hatsi a Niger. Suna hana tsutsa, beraye, da ruwa gaba ɗaya. Suna ɗaukar daga kilo 100 zuwa tan ɗaya ko fiye. Yin amfani da silos na iya kare hatsi har shekara guda ko fiye ba tare da hasara ba. Ana iya sayen silos ta hanyar ƙungiyoyin manoma (coopératives) ko tare da taimako daga hukumomi.

### Jakunan Hermetic (Hermetic Bags / PICS Bags)

Jakunan hermetic (kamar PICS bags wanda aka ƙirƙiro shi musamman don Niger da Sahel) suna rufe iska da ke cikin jakar gaba ɗaya. Idan iska ta ƙare, kwari dake cikin jakar sun mutu cikin kwana 7 zuwa 14. Yadda ake amfani: cika jaka a cika, cire iska gaba ɗaya ta murɗe bakin jakar kuma daure shi sosai. Kada a buɗe sai lokacin da ake buƙata. PICS bags suna da tsada kaɗan amma suna ɗaukar hatsi lafiya har shekara 2 zuwa 3.

### Rumbu na Gargajiya da Ganyen Lemu Daji (Traditional Storage with Neem)

Idan ba a da silos ko hermetic bags, ana iya amfani da rumbu na gargajiya (rumbu na laka ko zane) tare da ganyen lemu daji (neem leaves / Azadirachta indica). Hada ganyen lemu daji (mai ƙanshi mai ƙarfi) tare da hatsi a layi-layi: katami na hatsin, katami na ganyen, har a kammala adana. Ƙanshi na ganyen lemu daji na tsoratar da tsutsa kuma yana iya rage hasara. Wannan hanyar ba ta kiyaye kamar silos, amma ta fi babu komai.

### Shawarwari na Adana

Duba hatsin da aka adana kowane mako 2. Nema alamu kamar: ƙanshi mai ban mamaki, gari mai yawa a ƙasan ajiya (tsintsiyar tsutsa), ko hatsi mai laushi. Idan ka gano tsutsa da wuri, cire hatsin da aka kama, bushe shi a rana, a sake adana. Kiyaye ajiya a nisa daga ruwaye da ƙasar da ta yi damshi. Ƙofar ajiya ta rufe sosai don kare daga beraye.
