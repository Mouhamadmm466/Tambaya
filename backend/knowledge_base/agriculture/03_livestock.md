# Shanun Gida a Niger

## Awaki (Goats)

Awaki su ne dabbobi mafi yawan gaske a Niger. Suna da sauƙin kulawa kuma suna iya rayuwa da abinci kaɗan. Farashi a kasuwa: ɗan akuya (yaran akuya) mai watanni 6: CFA 15,000 zuwa 25,000. Akuya babba (mace): CFA 35,000 zuwa 60,000. Bunsuru (akuya namiji babba): CFA 40,000 zuwa 80,000. Farashi na tashi sosai a lokacin Sallah (Eid al-Adha) inda bunsuru mai kyau na iya kai CFA 100,000 ko fiye.

Cuta mafi hatsari ga awaki: PPR (Peste des Petits Ruminants), ana kiranta "cuta ta kashe awaki." Alamominta: zazzabi mai ƙarfi, gudawa, ƙuraje a bakin, hawaye da hancibakin ya kama. Yi allurar rigakafi ta PPR a kowace shekara. Kiyaye awaki masu rashin lafiya daga sauran dabbobi.

## Tumaki (Sheep)

Rago (tumaki namiji): CFA 30,000 zuwa 70,000. Tumaki mace babba: CFA 25,000 zuwa 55,000. Kamar awaki, farashin tumaki na tashi sosai a lokacin Sallah. Tumaki sun fi awaki ƙarfin ɗaukar nauyin fari. Su ma suna bukatar allurar rigakafi ta PPR kowace shekara. Kula da tumaki da ciyar da su busasshiyar ciyawa da hatsin da aka cire (bran) yayin damari.

## Shanu (Cattle)

Shanu suna da darajar kuɗi mafi girma daga duk dabbobin gona. Sa (bijimi/bijemi): CFA 300,000 zuwa 700,000 dangane da girma da kiba. Saniya (shanu mace da take da nono): CFA 200,000 zuwa 500,000. Maraƙi (ɗan shanu ƙarami): CFA 80,000 zuwa 180,000.

Cuta ta CBPP (Contagious Bovine Pleuropneumonia) cuta ce ta huhu da take shafar shanu. Alamominta: tari, wahalar numfashi, zazzabi. Yi allurar rigakafi kowace shekara. Kiyaye shanu a wurare masu iska mai kyau. Ka tabbatar shanu suna sha ruwa mai tsabta akai-akai, musamman a lokacin rani.

## Kaji (Chickens / Poultry)

Kaji na gida (local breeds): CFA 3,000 zuwa 6,000 kowane. Kaji na ingantaccen nau'i (improved breeds kamar Sasso): CFA 8,000 zuwa 18,000. Ƙwai: CFA 100 zuwa 200 kowane.

Cuta ta ya'am (Newcastle Disease) ita ce babbar musibar kaji. Tana iya kashe kaji da yawa cikin kwanaki kaɗan. Alamominta: kaji suna bullo, fikafikan na faɗuwa, ba sa ci abinci, mutuwa da sauri. Yi allurar rigakafi ta Newcastle a duk bayan watanni 3 zuwa 4. Kiyaye kaji a wurin da ya bushe kuma yana da iska. Cire kaji matattu da sauri don hana yaɗuwar cuta.

## Farashin Kasuwa da Lokacin Siyarwa

Mafi kyawun lokacin siyar da dabbobi shine kafin Sallah (Eid al-Adha da Eid al-Fitr) inda farashi ya fi tashi. Kasuwannin manyan biranen kamar Niamey, Zinder, Maradi, da Tahoua suna da farashi mafi tsada fiye da kasuwannin ƙauye. Lokacin rani (Maris zuwa Yuni) farashi kan ragu domin yawancin manoma na siyar da dabbobi saboda buƙatar kuɗi. Ana iya adana darajar dabbobi ta hanyar kula da lafiyarsu da ciyar da su da kyau.
