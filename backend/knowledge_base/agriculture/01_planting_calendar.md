# Kalandar Noma a Niger

Lokacin damina a Niger yana farawa daga watan Yuni zuwa watan Satumba. Wannan shi ne lokacin da ake noman hatsi da kayan lambu. Yankunan kudu na Niger suna samun ruwa fiye da yankunan arewa. Idan ruwan sama ya fara fita akai-akai, to lokacin shuka ya yi.

## Gero (Pearl Millet)

Gero shine amfanin gona mafi muhimmanci a Niger. Ana shuka gero da farkon ruwan sama, kusan farkon watan Yuni ko tsakiyar Yuni. Kada a jira har ruwan sama ya zama mai yawa sosai kafin shuka. Ana shuka gero a cikin ramuka masu nisan santimita 75 zuwa 80 a tsakanin juna. Ana sanya tsaba 3 zuwa 4 a cikin kowane rami, sannan a rufe da ƙasa mai laushi. Ana girbi gero a watan Satumba zuwa farkon watan Oktobar, kusan bayan kwana 90 zuwa 120 na shuka.

## Dawa (Sorghum)

Ana shuka dawa tsakanin tsakiyar watan Yuni da farkon watan Yuli. Dawa tana iya jimre da ɗan fari fiye da gero. Ana shuka dawa a ramuka masu nisan santimita 60 zuwa 75 a tsakanin juna. Ana sanya tsaba 2 zuwa 3 a kowane rami. Ana girbi dawa a watan Oktobar zuwa Nuwamba, kusan bayan kwana 100 zuwa 140 na shuka.

## Wake (Cowpeas / Black-eyed Peas)

Ana shuka wake a watan Yuli. Za a iya shuka wake a tsakanin layin gero ko dawa (intercropping), ko a shuka shi shi kaɗai. Wake yana inganta ƙasa ta hanyar ƙara nitrojen daga iska zuwa cikin ƙasa. Ana girbi wake a watan Satumba zuwa Oktobar.

## Gyada (Groundnuts / Peanuts)

Ana shuka gyada a watan Yuni ko farkon Yuli, a lokacin da ƙasa ta ji danshi sosai. Gyada na bukatar ƙasa mai yashi da laushi domin 'ya'yanta su iya girma cikin ƙasa lafiya. Ana shuka a ramuka masu nisan santimita 40 zuwa 50 a tsakanin. Ana girbi gyada a watan Satumba zuwa Oktobar.

## Ridi (Sesame)

Ana shuka ridi a watan Yuli bayan ruwan sama ya yi tsayi. Ridi na iya rayuwa da ruwa kaɗan kuma yana da ƙarfi wajen jimre da fari. Ana girbi ridi a watan Oktobar. Ridi yana da daraja sosai a kasuwanni saboda mai da za a iya fitarwa daga gare shi.

## Shawarwari na Lokacin Shuka

Shuka da wuri yana taimaka wajen guje wa kwari da cututtuka. Idan aka shuka da ɗan jinkiri (bayan tsakiyar Yuli), yawan amfanin gona na ragu. Duba yanayin ƙasa kafin shuka: idan ka ture yatsan hannu cikin ƙasa santimita 5 kuma ƙasa ta yi danshi, to ƙasa ta shirya don shuka.
